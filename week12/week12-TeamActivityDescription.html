WDD 330

Ponder: Team Activity

Overview

This activity will give you an opportunity to share what you are doing with your teammates...and hopefully get some feedback or suggestions at the same time. This weeks activity will probably take less than an hour.

Instructions

Complete the following assignment as a team. Designate one team member as the "main driver" and collaborate on their copy of the code. Everyone on the team should be actively engaged in writing the code and contributing to the solution. Once the solution is working, make sure that everyone on the team gets a copy of the code. Each week let someone else be the "main driver" of the coding.

Core Requirements

  1. Each team member should share something they are working on with their challenge. This can be something you are currently working on, are stuck on, are pleased with how it turned out, etc.

Stretch Goals

  1. Each team member should give feedback to one or more other members of the team.

Instructors Solution

No instructors Solution on this one! Check yes on the assessment for reviewing the instructor's code.

Submission

When you have finished this activity, please fill out the assessment in I-Learn. You are welcome to complete any additional parts of this activity by yourself or with others after your meeting before submitting the assessment.
